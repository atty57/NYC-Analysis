NYC Vehicle Collision Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of motor vehicle collisions in New York City

Annual Collision Trends

Key Features

  • Visualization of serious collisions and fatalities
  • Integration with NYC traffic data
  • Interactive maps and detailed statistics
